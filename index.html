Hack-a-thon

A mobile location app for Firefighters

I worked with a team of programmers to make this mobile app for the IoTCivivHack sponsored by AT&T to work with emergency responders. The goal of the app was to use google maps API to tag the latitude and longitude of fire hydrants as well as their condition and specifications for accessing - helping firefighters save time when they need it most.
